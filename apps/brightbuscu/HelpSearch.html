Help Search

What is Search?


  • Search is used to find bus stops.

How do I use Search?


  • Use search to look up stop codes to add to your favorites. Use the search box to enter the name of an intersection or location, the tap "Search". If there is more than one stop that matches your search, tap the correct stop. The stop page will show you buses arriving soon. Tap the "+" button on the top toolbar to add this stop to your favorites.

Are there any tips for searching?


  • Use letters instead of numbers ("fourth" instead of "4th").
  • Search for intersections using "and" or a comma ("green and white" or "green, white").
  • Search for locations ("meijer").
  • Try single street names and choose the correct cross street after ("goodwin").
